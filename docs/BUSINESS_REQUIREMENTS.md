# Business Requirements — lore-eligibility

> **Status:** Skeleton. Populated in Phase 01 (BRD authorship).

This document is the formal Business Requirements Document derived from
`DOCUMENTS/PROBLEM_BRIEF.md`. It is the input to the ARD
(`docs/ARCHITECTURE.md`).

The PROBLEM_BRIEF captures the problem space and explicit case-prompt
deliverables. The BRD translates those into:

1. **Functional requirements** — what the system must do, expressed
   as testable capabilities
2. **Non-functional requirements** — quantified performance, reliability,
   security, and operational targets
3. **Out-of-scope items** — explicit non-goals, with rationale
4. **Acceptance criteria** — what "done" means for each requirement
